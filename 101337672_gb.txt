George Brown Collage is a public Collage located in Downtown Toronto. 
George Brown Collage has three compuses. 
Specifically, Casa Loma Compus is located at 160 Kendal Ave, Toronto.
George Brown Collage is know for many of its programs such as Computer 
Programming and Analysis, Cyber Security, Finance and Human Resources. 
There are many other programs that can fit anyones preferences and choices. 
It's also one of the most known Collages of Toronto. When looked closely, 
Goerge Brown Collage not only grows as a Collage but also through the diverse 
community it is having. Being part of this community is a key marker of both 
students and employees success. Community helps bread not only academic and classroom 
community success but also success in organization and connection to foreign relations.
