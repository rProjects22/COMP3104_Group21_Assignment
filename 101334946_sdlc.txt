SDLC
---------------------
SDLC also known as Software Development Life Cycle. This process consist of 7 phases which is Requirement Analysis, Feasibility Study,
Design, Coding, Testing, Install/Deploy, and Maintenance. This process helps us develop a high quality software by making our work cycle 
efficient. Its important for DevOps engineer to follow the SDLC as it acts as a guide on how to tackle the project, helps in scheduling 
deliverables, and making sure that clients receive their product in a timely fashion.


Learning devops helps reducing the time cycles of development.
It innovates faster
