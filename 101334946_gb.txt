
George Brown College
---------------------
George Brown College is a public college chartered in 1966 and established at 1967. Officially opened March of 1968. 
It is the combination of  Provincial Institute of Trades and the Provincial Institute of Trades and Occupations that 
formed the George Brown College. C.C. Lloyd is the first principal of the college.

Currently George Brown College has three main campuses from Casa Loma, St. James and Waterfront campus. 
Each campus has a special program that they specialize in. These programs can range from computer programming, nursing, culinary and etc.

For more information about George Brown College
Please visit https://www.georgebrown.ca/
